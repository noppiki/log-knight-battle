HOW TO PLAY

GOAL

Knock your opponent off the log first. Best of 3 rounds wins the match.

CONTROLS

PLAYER 1 (CRIMSON)

  • A / D   scoot left / right
  • W   hop forward
  • S   guard (raise pan)
  • SPACE   swing pan (tap rapidly)
  • F   special move

PLAYER 2 (AZURE)

  • ◄ ►   scoot left / right
  • ▲   hop forward
  • ▼   guard
  • ENTER   swing pan
  • /   special move

Mobile: on-screen buttons appear during play.

BALANCE = LIFE

The top bar is your life. Take hits to lose balance — at 0 (or shoved past the log edge), you tumble off and lose the round.

SP GAUGE & SPECIALS

The thin gauge below your life bar fills via combat. When full, press your SPECIAL key (F or "/") to unleash the move you picked at the start of the match.

MODES

  • 1P vs CPU — solo against AI of medium difficulty.
  • 2P LOCAL — two players on the same keyboard.
  • ONLINE — host or join a room over the relay.
  • TRAINING — infinite life + passive dummy; practice freely.

TIPS

  • The pendulum has momentum — rapid taps build a bigger swing.
  • Guarding halves incoming knockback but you can't swing during it.
  • Near the edge you get pushed harder. The center is safer.
  • Press P or Esc to pause anytime (offline only).
  • Press O for SETTINGS (volume, shake, KO slow-mo).
